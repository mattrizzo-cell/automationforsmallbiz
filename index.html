Our AI Assessment Process

  1. Offer AI Assessments: We provide tailored AI evaluations for small business owners.
  2. 45-Minute Zoom Interview: We "interview" you to identify your biggest bottlenecks and pain points.
  3. Record with Fathom: Calls are recorded for accurate analysis.
  4. LLM Analysis: Transcripts are fed to advanced AI to recommend off-the-shelf tools that solve your issues.
  5. Polished Report with Gamma: We create a professional report summarizing the findings and recommendations.
  6. 30-Minute Review Call: Schedule a call to review the report and discuss implementation options.
